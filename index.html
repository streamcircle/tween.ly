Supporting broadcasting creativity

HTML and JS Animation Tool for Broadcast Graphics

Tweenly, developed by Provys Technologies, is the HTML and JS animation tool designed to simplify the workflow for both designers and broadcasters. It seamlessly integrates HTML-based graphics with broadcast playout systems, allowing designers to create visually stunning broadcast graphics.

Visual design without coding

Create and customize graphics effortlessly using an intuitive drag-and-drop editor, no coding required.

Image sequence management

Seamlessly process and manage image sequences for dynamic visual content.

Real-time data integration

Incorporate live data feeds directly into your graphics for up-to-the-minute updates.

Collaborative shared workspaces

Work together in real-time with your team, ensuring smooth collaboration within shared environments.

Solution

Get more of your animation process

Take full control of your broadcast graphics design and simplify the process from start to finish. Tweenly allows you to spend more time creating dynamic tickers, lower thirds and titles, and less time dealing with technical challenges.

Editor

The editor scene consists of shape objects, images, and texts organized and nested in hierarchical structures and styled via CSS. Interactive timeline and scene preview provide real-time feedback on the appearance of all animated objects in various time stamps.

Workspaces

Creating rich brand graphics doesn't have to be a one-man show. Workspaces were designed to make graphics accessible to all its members for collaborative creation and management.

Gallery

Tweenly comes with cloud storage for access to the graphics on your personal account. For graphics transfer automation, the gallery can be integrated with content management solutions through API.

Store

You may overflow with creativity worth sharing or, on the contrary, look for attractive graphical outputs of others to use for your own purposes. The store provides space for the meeting of offers and demands of globally available graphics. Some are free, others are valued with a certain amount of our coin transaction system.
