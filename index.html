Supporting broadcasting creativity

Modern HTML and JS animation tool

Tweenly was developed by Stream Circle to provide a broadcasting graphical tool that is understandable for every creative person. This web application solution with a powerful editor at its core focuses on the production of graphics in HTML format to enrich any linear channel operations. With its cloud storage, shared workspaces, and a global graphics store, Tweenly strives for more than just another HTML editor.

Solution

Get more of your animation process

Editor

The editor scene consists of shape objects, images, and texts organized and nested in hierarchical structures and styled via CSS. Interactive timeline and scene preview provide real-time feedback on the appearance of all animated objects in various time stamps.

Workspaces

Creating rich brand graphics doesn't have to be a one-man show. Workspaces were designed to make graphics accessible to all its members for collaborative creation and management.

Gallery

Tweenly comes with cloud storage for access to the graphics on your personal account. For graphics transfer automation, the gallery can be integrated with content management solutions through API.

Store

You may overflow with creativity worth sharing or, on the contrary, look for attractive graphical outputs of others to use for your own purposes. The store provides space for the meeting of offers and demands of globally available graphics. Some are free, others are valued with a certain amount of our coin transaction system.
